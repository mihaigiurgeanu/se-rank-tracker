inventory
/ˈɪnv(ə)nt(ə)ri/
noun
a complete list of items such as property, goods in stock, or the contents of a building.
sinonime: list, listing, catalogue, directory, record, register, checklist, tally, roster, file, log, account, archive, description, statement

verb
make a complete list of.
„I inventoried his collection of drawings”
